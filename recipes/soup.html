Mushroom Soup

Rich and creamy Hungarian mushroom soup

This Hungarian mushroom soup has lots of flavor and is fairly quick to make. It's primarily a mushroom soup but derives a lot of its flavor from other ingredients. My family loves soup and this is one of their favorites. Let's get started!

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cups chopped onions
  • 1 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 2 teaspoons dried dill weed
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions; cook and stir until softened, about 5 minutes. Add mushrooms and saut√© for 5 more minutes. Stir in broth, soy sauce, paprika, and dill; reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes.
  2. Whisk milk and flour together in a separate bowl; stir into soup until blended. Cover and simmer for 15 more minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add sour cream, parsley, lemon juice, salt, and ground black pepper; stir over low heat until warmed through, about 3 to 5 minutes. Serve immediately.
